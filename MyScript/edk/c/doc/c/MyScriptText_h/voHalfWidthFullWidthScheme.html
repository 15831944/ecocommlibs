#include <c/MyScriptText.h>
typedef enum _voHalfWidthFullWidthScheme voHalfWidthFullWidthScheme

Lists the half width / full width character conversion scheme values.

Since:

7.3_5

Value Summary
VO_DEFAULT_HALF_WIDTH_FULL_WIDTH
          No specific rule is applied to convert characters into their half width or full width forms.
VO_AUTO_HALF_WIDTH_FULL_WIDTH
          Smart rules are applied to convert symbols into their half width or full width forms depending on the context.
VO_FORCE_HALF_WIDTH
          Force conversion of characters into their half width forms if any.
VO_FORCE_FULL_WIDTH
          Force conversion of characters into their full width forms if any.
