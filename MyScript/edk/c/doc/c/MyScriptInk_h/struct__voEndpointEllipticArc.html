#include <c/MyScriptInk.h>
struct _voEndpointEllipticArc

Represents an elliptic arc on the 2D plane, by its endpoint parameterization.

Note:

In order to ensure bijective conversion between endpoint and center parameterizations, we interpret the degenerate case where the two endpoints are equal as follow: When p2 equals p1, they define the (1,0) point on the origin circle (i.e.: before stretch and rotate), and a full ellipse.
