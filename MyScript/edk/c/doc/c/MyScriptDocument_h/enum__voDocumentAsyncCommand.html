#include <c/MyScriptDocument.h>
enum __voDocumentAsyncCommand

DEPRECATED.

Lists the asynchronous commands of voDocument and voPage.

See also:
voDocumentAsyncCommandCompletionCallback
deprecated as of 8.0.0
