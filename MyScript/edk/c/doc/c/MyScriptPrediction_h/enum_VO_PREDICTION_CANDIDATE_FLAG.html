Enumeration VO_PREDICTION_CANDIDATE_FLAG

#include <c/MyScriptPrediction.h>
enum _VO_PREDICTION_CANDIDATE_FLAG

Lists the recognition result candidate flags for candidates added by the predictor.

Note:

All the candidates added by the predictor are also flagged with VO_CANDIDATE_FLAG_OMITTED.

See also:

Value Summary
VO_CANDIDATE_FLAG_COMPLETED= 1 << 8
          The candidate comes from the completion phase.
VO_CANDIDATE_FLAG_PREDICTED= 1 << 9
          The candidate comes from the prediction phase.
